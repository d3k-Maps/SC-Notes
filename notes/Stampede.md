## **Summary**

Stampede! is a chaotic survival‑racer where you charge forward through dense crowds and hazardous obstacles while a massive stampede bears down behind you. Your goal is simple: **reach the end before the herd catches you**.

### **Objective**
Stay ahead of the stampede and make it to the finish line.

### **Key Mechanics**
- **Plow through crowds and obstacles** to keep moving forward  
- **Collisions slow you down**, so choose your path wisely  
- **Lay mines** to offset speed loss when you inevitably hit something  
- **Watch the stampede behind you** — fall too far back and you're done  
- **Run over special targets** in the crowd to gain bonuses  
- **Survive the chaos and reach the end** to win  

### **Inspiration**
Plays similarly to **Oozing Blob**, with a focus on momentum, crowd navigation, and that constant pressure behind you.
