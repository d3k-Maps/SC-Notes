## Objectives

A sequel to the original [Escape From Prison](./EscapeFromPrison.scm). 

-----

## History

### **v1.13**
* Made the "last stretch" area more fun and less impossible.
* Added a third secret
* Added the Mad Scientist

### **v1.12**
* Corrected an issue where units were not properly given to Player 2.
* Applied a potential fix for the "Key 3" bug.
* Added an ending credits animation.
* Updated terrain, patrol orders, and miscellaneous text.
* Adjusted equipment triggers to allow a single player to use certain items twice.
* Fixed a rare glitch where units were unplaceable at the start of the game.
* Adjusted the "Center View" action to track the player's hero.

### **v1.11**
* Fixed a trigger that incorrectly allied Force 1 and Player 7 during the Boss Battle.
* Fixed a bug that killed tanks intended for Force 1.
* Updated various unit and location names.
* Increased Boss attack damage from 10 to 44 and improved Boss AI.

### **v1.10**
* Player 2 is now allied to Player 7 during the Boss Battle.
* Created the "Die Blue Things!" location to prevent viewing previous map areas.
* Removed the Map Revealer at the "Buff Guards" location.
* Temporarily granted control of units in the Secret Weapon Room and Last Stretch to Player 2.
* Added a "First to Beat" list to the Mission Briefing.
* Further modified Special Door opening triggers.

### **v1.09**
* Removed a faulty trigger that caused Player 2 (Blue) to reach the Defeat action erroneously.
* Removed and replaced mines in the Gas Chamber room.
* Added a missing Geyser behind the Academy.

### **v1.08**
* Implemented a theoretical fix for unpredictable Mine behavior.

### **v1.07**
* Corrected a misplaced Yellow trigger.
* Replaced a potentially corrupt switch.

### **v1.06**
* Modified the Special Door opening triggers.
* Increased the size of the "Frontier2" location.
* Replaced a Gas Chamber with a Dog Pen.

### **v1.05**
* Reduced Control Room wait time by 30 seconds.
* Adjusted defeat conditions: if the Hacker dies after successfully finishing the hack, the game no longer ends in failure.
* Relocated a blue Ghost positioned next to the Hacker.

### **v1.04**
* Fixed a logic bug in the Secret Door opening conditions.
* Increased the size of the Secret Rifles location.

### **v1.03**
* Fixed the "don't move" trigger action for Hackers.
* Prevented a bug where players could get stuck in the "Create Guard Dogs" room.

### **v1.02**
* Fixed the door explosion glitch occurring with multiple players.
* Increased the size of the Secret Key location.
* Added backup AI-controlled Ghosts to the Control Room.
* Secret Rifles are now created in the Barracks area.
* Rations are now created in the Control Room.
* Blocked access to the Scourge area after the Secret Key is obtained.

### **v1.01**
* Fixed various text display flaws.
* Updated the Control Room section to be playable by 3 players.
* Added a secret method to skip the Scourge section.
* Scourges now die upon being hit.
* Improved triggers for acquiring weapons.
* The Boss now displays a health bar/status.
* Implemented the "Recorded Update" tracking system.
