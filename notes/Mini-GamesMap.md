## Games

There are 19 mini-games you can play:

* **Maze**
    * **Objective**: Find the exit.

* **Glitchy Wall Jump**
    * **Objective**: Jump over all the crystals without bumping into any purple crystals 

* **Pattern Puzzle**
    * **Objective**: Kill the correct civilians in the right order to pass; killing the wrong civilian resets the puzzle 

* **Fire Path**
    * **Objective**: Reach the end of the path without touching any of the fire 

* **Lure**
    * **Objective**: Lure all archons into explosions. Attacking them makes them run away, which can be used to your advantage. If they get stuck in corners, use the "burrowing trick"

* **Perfect Smiley**
    * **Objective**: Use exactly 11 pylons to create a perfect smiley face consisting only of eyes and a mouth 

* **Blood Bath**
    * **Objective**: Kill all burrowed creatures by attacking your medic 

* **Stimulation**
    * **Objective**: Move your Zergling back and forth between two areas to gain eggs. Moving faster yields more eggs, and you must collect all eggs to win 

* **Yellow Rines**
    * **Objective**: Watch a group of Marines and count how many flash yellow. Indicate your guess by sieging your tank; the number of minerals you have represents your guess

* **Mine Field**
    * **Objective**: Place at least 100 mines in the designated area. Bumping into your own mines will cause them to explode

* **Lurker Splash**
    * **Objective**: Kill 5 Dark Templars (DTs) as they spawn from the right and move to the left. It takes two hits from your Lurker's splash damage to kill one DT

* **BullzEye**
    * **Objective**: Siege your tank exactly when a red explosion occurs. You must do this 5 times to win

* **Dodge The Ling**
    * **Objective**: Avoid roaming Zerglings to gain up to 1,000 minerals. Bumping into a Zergling results in a penalty of 15 minerals

* **5 Bounds**
    * **Objective**: Dodge explosions to reach the end of five different obstacle levels. If you die, you automatically respawn

* **Collectin Scouts**
    * **Objective**: Bump into Scouts to gain points while avoiding Wraiths, which subtract points. You need 10 points to win

* **Missile Launching**
    * **Objective**: Use your Defiler's Dark Swarm to shoot missiles at surrounding enemy units. The missiles do not follow targets and only explode upon reaching their destination

* **Warm & Cold**
    * **Objective**: Move around the area to find a hidden Infested Terran. The game uses a "warm" and "cold" feedback system (indicated by Corsair and Zealot visual cues) to let you know how close you are to the target 

* **Mixture Madness**
    * **Objective**: Kill Lurkers to manage your resources. Killing a player 7 Lurker adds gas and subtracts ore, while killing a player 8 Lurker adds ore and subtracts gas

* **The Cave of Doom**
    * **Objective**: Survive and navigate through a "cave" area while avoiding or dealing with obstacles and enemy units. The code mentions "The Cave of Doom" in the context of running AI scripts and spawning Drones

* **Invisible Path**
    * **Objective**: Navigate to the end of a path where the walkway is not visible. Touching the sides will reset your position.

* **Obstacle Course**
    * **Objective**: Reach the end of the course while dodging moving units and traps
