## 🎮 **Guide**
- **Unload units** to perform actions as a Racer  
- Use **Speed Up** and **Slow Down** to adjust speed (shown as **gas**)  
- **Corners are key** — use **Turn** to initiate diagonal movement on corners
- Higher speed (**gas**) = **longer turn duration** (shown as **ore**)  
- Max speed is **11**, but you can only turn at **10 or below** (with one exception)  
- Crashing resets your speed to 0 — use **Jump Start** to recover faster  
- **Jump Start** can also act as a **hard brake**, reducing speed and slightly shortening turn length  
- **Switch Lanes** to set up better corner angles or land well‑timed bumps  
- You can only **bump** at **speed 6+** and only after the first turn  
- Drafting behind another racer gives:
  - A speed boost  
  - Easier turning  
  - Temporary ability to turn at **speed 11**  
- Use **Perspective settings** to toggle between:
  - **Free Focus**  
  - **Hard Focus**  
  - **Spectate** (after elimination)

## 🎮 **Game Modes**

### **Knockout**
The racer in **last place each lap is eliminated** until only one remains.

---

### **Practice**
A free‑form mode where you can **drive, experiment, and test mechanics**.  
The first practice provides a interactive **Tutorial**. Exit practice at the **top‑right** of the map.

---

### **Standard**
A classic tournament style race. **Top 4** advance. Then **Top 2**. With **Top 1** as winner.
Or choose to compete in one race for **Top 3** and receive a medal.

---

### **Time Trial**
Each racer takes turns attempting to complete a number of laps **as fast as possible**.  
Best time wins. Or choose to eliminate the worst time each round.

## 💡 **Random Tips**

### **Tip #1**  
Slow down before turning. Only your **initial speed** affects turn length.

### **Tip #2**  
You can **speed up mid‑turn** without increasing the turn duration.

### **Tip #3**  
You can only unload units so fast — time rapid actions carefully.

### **Tip #4**  
In **Free Focus**, center your screen on the next corner.  
In **Hard Focus**, rely on terrain cues.

### **Tip #5**  
Hug the inside corner or start wide to give yourself more room to complete a turn.

### **Tip #6**  
Turning is much easier at **speed 8** (turn length 12) than at **9** (15) or **10** (16).  
Turn length = **minerals (ore)**.

### **Tip #7**  
Most racers lose by crashing. Drive safely and you’ll outperform many players.

### **Tip #8**  
Straightaways are perfect for hitting **speed 10 or 11** — but remember, you **cannot turn on 11**.

### **Tip #9**  
Use **Switch Lanes** to move away from the inside corner and set up wider, safer turns.

### **Tip #10**  
If someone is faster, slow down and let them pass — then use **Switch Lanes** to bump them at the right moment.

### **Tip #11**  
Drafting triggers when you enter another racer’s trail explosion.  
It boosts speed, eases turning, and even lets you turn at **speed 11** briefly.

### **Tip #12**  
After crashing, **Jump Start** quickly gets you from speed 0 → 3.  
At high speeds, it instead **reduces speed by 4** and slightly shortens turn length.

### **Tip #13**  
A fast **Jump Start** at the beginning of a race can boost you from speed 0 → 5.

### **Tip #14**  
Try **holding Turn early** before reaching a corner.  
Then release at the right moment — reduces misclicks and improves reaction timing.

### **Tip #15**  
Seeing **lurker blood** after a crash means you were close to a successful turn.  
Next time, adjust by starting earlier or taking a wider angle.
