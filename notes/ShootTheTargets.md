## Objectives

A sequel to the original [Shoot Da Targets](./notes/ShootDaTargets.md), your goal is to shoot down all the targets. The amount of targets and the distance from them increases each level. Unlike the first game, the amount of ammunition and targets is much greater. Earn special guns from target hit streaks and milestones throughout the game.
