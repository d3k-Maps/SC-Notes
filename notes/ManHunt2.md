## Objectives

A sequel to the original [Man Hunt](./notes/ManHunt.md). Look for suspicious activity in the crowds of people. Prevent **Terrorists** from reaching the airport, **Drug Dealers** from spreading their drugs, **Suicide Bombers** from exploding, and **Serial Killers** from killing indiscriminately. 

Eliminate all threats to win.
